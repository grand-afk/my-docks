Help
🔍 Finding & saving stations
Tap 📍 Nearby to find the 10 closest stations to your current location. Or tap 🔍 Search, type a street or station name, and tap ＋ Save on any result.
✏️ Editing your list
Tap Edit to enter edit mode. Tap ✕ to remove a station. Press and hold the ⠿ handle and drag to reorder. Tap ✓ Done when finished.
🚲 Cycling directions
Tap Cycle Here on any card to open cycling directions to that station — Apple Maps on iPhone, Google Maps elsewhere.
⇅ Syncing between devices
Your stations are saved in this browser only. To copy them to another device:

1. Tap ⇅ Sync → ⬇ Download config or ⎘ Copy to clipboard
2. On the other device, tap ⇅ Sync → ⬆ Import config or paste the JSON
📱 Add to iPhone home screen
In Safari, tap the Share button (box with arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add. It will appear as an app icon.
